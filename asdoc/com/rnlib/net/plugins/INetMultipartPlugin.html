Packagecom.rnlib.net.plugins
Interfacepublic interface INetMultipartPlugin extends INetPlugin, flash.events.IEventDispatcher
Implementors MultipartFilePlugin

Developer of MultipartPlugin is responsible to dispatch event Event.READY always after execute by RemoteAmfService init() or next() method. Please notice that init() method is calling only once at start "lifecycle" of plugin. Method next() is calling as many times as needed to complete work by plugin. After that plugin dispatch event NetPluginEvent.COMPLETE
